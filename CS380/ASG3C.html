Oak Wood Variety

Oak Wood is some of the strongest wood we sale. Coming in with a density of 0.75 g/cubic centimeter,
it is great for those projects that need the strength with a small space. One specific project is building
whiskey barrels. Oak has a high concentration of tannin which increases its resistance to insects and and
fungal attacks. Throughout history, Oak was used to build homes, ships, and storage containers. Today,
you can purchase these branches from us and enjoy building a durable, stable Oak Wood Project.

Athens State University has been one of our prime customers. Below, you will see how some of our
Oak Branches have made it into the academic world. This is their current project to Build a Statue to
honor the University's tradition of the Fiddler's Convention held every October at
Athens State University.
