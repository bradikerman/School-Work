Business Analysis and Reporting

By looking at external and internal factors, market dynamics, and performance across all levels of your business, Cybernetic Retail is able to provide just the information you need to strengthen and improve your presence in the retail marketplace. The challenge in today's industry isn't lack of data, it's too much data. We help by identifying your company’s strengths, weaknesses and key opportunities and presenting the data in a useful and actionable format. Our goal is to work with our clients to ensure they have the most meaningful information available to assist in managing their company's future.

Epicor Integration

As an Epicor Services Partner with a large team of certified Epicor developers, we can be your single source for any custom Epicor integration services for your Epicor 9 ERP, 10 or Vantage implementation. Whether you need web integration, eCommerce or some other application integrated with Epicor, give us a call and lets chat about your needs.

Omni-Channel Platforms

Omni-channel retailers provide consumers the ability to experience an entire brand, not just a segment of a brand. By utilizing brick and mortar stores as extensions of your service offering, customers can research their purchases through other channels allowing consumers the ability to tailor their shopping experience for themselves.

E-Commerce Solutions

Today's retailers need to plan not only for their brick and mortar stores, but they must also have an exceptional e-commerce platform to engage their customers. Retail e-commerce sites are a vital method of getting more value from brick and mortar stores, chain inventory, and ongoing customer loyalty.

Warehouse Management Systems

A superior warehouse management system is a key part of any effective supply chain, maximizing aspects from warehouse tracking systems to real-time product movement and streamlined order picking. Our consultants offer insights into increased inventory accuracy utilizing proven systems to manage changes in order volume, distribution points and complex fulfillment logistics.

In-Store Dashboards

In-store Dashboards are a powerful tool that enables organizations to gain better, faster insight into invaluable retail metrics and performance. They can be used to help analyze company data at various levels from product, to store, to region, and turn that data into real-time actionable decision making. Our dashboard options can be used in conjunction with performance reporting and business intelligence analysis to obtain tangible ROIs.

Allocation and Replenishment Optimization

In a market where retailers are compelled to adjust mid-stream to ensure product availability, efficient A&R allows retailers to adapt to changes across retail environments or store performances. A powerful allocation and replenishment solution allows companies to react to trends in the marketplace, while maintaining the ability to manage inventory on an ongoing basis without disruption to the rest of your chain. The Cybernetic Retail Allocation & Replenishment options support clients in defining A&R strategies, develop successful inventory methodologies and build optimized long-term solutions throughout your chain.
